Required Visa Documents Checklist (Visit Visa)

Documents Needed for UK Visa Application

  • Valid CNIC Scan (Both sides)
  • Passport Scan (Front page, last page, and previous visa pages)
  • Recent Passport Size Photograph (UK-standard specifications)
  • Family Registration Certificate (FRC) or Marriage Registration Certificate (MRC)
  • Police Clearance Certificate (PCC)
  • Personal Bank Statement (Last 6 months, stamped by the bank)
  • Bank Account Maintenance Letter
  • FBR NTN Certificate
  • Tax Return Documents (Last 3 years, if applicable)
  • Employment Letter or Business Registration Documents
  • Proof of Accommodation (Hotel booking or Invitation letter)
  • Detailed Travel Itinerary
